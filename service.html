Wheel Balancing

Car Wheel Alignment: Ensuring a Smoother and Safer Ride Car wheel alignment plays a crucial role in the performance and safety of your vehicle. It refers to the process of adjusting the angles of the wheels to manufacturer specifications, ensuring that they are parallel to each other and perpendicular to the ground. Proper wheel alignment not only ensures a smoother ride but also enhances tire lifespan, steering response, and fuel efficiency.
